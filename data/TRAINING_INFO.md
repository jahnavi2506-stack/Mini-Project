# Training Information

## ✅ Yes, Dataset is Trained and Used!

### How Training Works:

1. **Sample Dataset (CSV):**
   - When you click "Try Sample Dataset", it loads `data/sample_transactions.csv`
   - **Automatically trains** ML models with the 20 sample transactions
   - Models are ready to use immediately!

2. **Your Own Data:**
   - When you add transactions through the website
   - Models **automatically train** when you have 2+ transactions
   - No manual training needed!

3. **Flexible Training:**
   - **No hard 10-transaction requirement anymore!**
   - Works with **any amount of data** you have
   - Trains with 2+ transactions (minimum for basic models)
   - More data = better accuracy, but it works with whatever you have

---

## Training Requirements (Flexible):

### Minimum Requirements:
- **Anomaly Detection:** 2+ transactions
- **Expense Analysis:** 2+ transactions  
- **Categorization:** 2+ labeled transactions (with categories)
- **Savings Suggestions:** Works with any data (needs income info)

### What Gets Trained:

1. **Anomaly Detector:**
   - Learns your spending patterns
   - Detects unusual transactions
   - Works with 2+ transactions

2. **Expense Analyzer:**
   - Identifies spending patterns by category
   - Suggests savings opportunities
   - Works with 2+ transactions

3. **Transaction Categorizer:**
   - Learns to categorize transactions automatically
   - Needs labeled transactions (with categories)
   - Works with 2+ labeled transactions

4. **Savings Analyzer:**
   - Analyzes income vs spending
   - Suggests savings goals
   - Works with any data (needs income info)
